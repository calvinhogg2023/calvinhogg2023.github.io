Classroom

August 2022

ABOUT THIS PIECE

After seeing how well Mount Victoria turned out, I decided to recreate a more familiar location. This is an unfinished render of the classroom I spent late nights working in during my final year. This made it a convenient choice as I was able to make the proportions accurate with a tape measure. The scene extends outside, revealing a carpark and alley.

I had several ideas for ways to extend this project, for instance in a virtual reality experience or a short film featuring classmates.

Mount Victoria

June 2022

ABOUT THIS PIECE

This render was part of an experiment involving photogrammetry, a technique involving capturing dozens of photos to generate a textured 3D model. I used close to a thousand photos of an area of Mount Victoria, resulting in a convenient base mesh.

I then created new clean geometry with the right proportions, and dropped the Dylan character into the scene.

My Name Is Sole Letafu

April 2022

ABOUT THIS PIECE

While learning photogrammetry, I took several photos of Dylan but didn't tell him what I planned to do with them.

I brought the images into RealityCapture, then unwrapped the texture and applied it to the Dylan V1 model. After some basic facial rigging involving blendshapes, I synced this animation to a voice clip and showed the result to the class.

The resemblance was incredible, but I don't think this model was blushing enough.

Major Projects Game

June 2022

ABOUT THIS PIECE

My major project for semester 1 was a video game and console, reminiscent of the Nintendo DS. The game would be of the exploration genre, featuring physical representations of my work. For instance, photography would be displayed in a gallery, while the Cyberthug Core from 2021 would be life-size with fancy effects.

I had planned to build the console using Raspberry Pi along with a combination of lasercutting and 3D printing techniques. The operating system for the console would be a stripped down version of Ubuntu with a custom made homescreen, however due to time limits I wasn't able to complete this.
