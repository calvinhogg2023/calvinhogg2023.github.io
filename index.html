About me

Hi, I'm Calvin. Welcome to my website. You might be wondering why I'm posting my work here and not on social media. You see, I studied digital media, and presentation is important. Why be suffocated by the limitations of another platform and the distractions of other artists' work, when I could make my own site which is a work of art in and of itself?


I hold a Bachelor's degree in Digital Media which I completed at the Wellington Institute of Technology, and a Certificate in Information Technology, completed at Whitireia. I have several years of experience with Adobe Creative Cloud and Autodesk Maya, and know a little about photography and programming.
