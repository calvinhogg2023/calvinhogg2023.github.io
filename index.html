Welcome

I'm Calvin. I built this portfolio to showcase a truly personalized exhibition of my work, rather than be bound by the limitations of social media sites. My area of interest is CGI and animation, but I've dabbled with photography and game development too.


I originally studied programming back in 2016, but realized I was more interested in the artistic aspect of my current field. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Digital Media, which I completed at the Wellington Institute of Technology in 2022, and a Certificate in Information Technology, completed at Whitireia.


When I'm not in front of the computer, I enjoy a good coffee on a cold day, or an energy drink if I'm feeling extra bold.
