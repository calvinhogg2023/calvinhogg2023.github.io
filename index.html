About me

Hi, I'm Calvin. Welcome to my website.

My passions include modelling 3D characters and environments, photography, a good coffee and walks in nature.

I have several years of experience with photography and 3D modelling, and am skilled with software packages like Adobe Creative Cloud and Autodesk Maya.
