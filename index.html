About me

Hi, I'm Calvin. Welcome to my website.

My passions include modelling 3D characters and environments, photography, a good coffee and walks in nature.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Digital Media which I completed at the Wellington Institute of Technology, and a Certificate in Information Technology, completed at Whitireia. I have several years of experience with photography and 3D modelling, and am skilled with software packages like Adobe Creative Cloud and Autodesk Maya.
