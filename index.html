Welcome

I'm Calvin. I built this portfolio to showcase a truly personalized exhibition of my work, rather than be bound by the limitations of social media sites. I consider my specialty to be CGI and animation, but I know a wee bit about photography and game development too.


I originally studied Information Technology back in 2016, but realized I was more interested in the artistic aspect of my current field. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Digital Media, which I completed at the Wellington Institute of Technology in 2022.


When I'm not in front of the computer, I enjoy a good coffee on a cold day, or an energy drink if I'm feeling extra bold.
